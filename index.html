Player Types

  • Burst Dribbler: Carries the ball forward quickly to gain ground.
  • Dynamic Playmaker: Moves the ball quickly to teammates to start attacks.
  • Power Dribbler: Uses strength to carry the ball through opponents.
  • Anchor Playmaker: Uses strength and composure to control play and distribute the ball.
  • Front-Footer: Tries to win the ball early by stepping forward and applying pressure.
  • Ball-Hawk Diffuser: Aggressively hunts down opponents to stop attacks and win possession.
  • Dvavser Regista: Stays deeper, reads the game, and reacts to danger by intercepting play.
  • Retalia: Waits for opponents to make a move, then tackles or challenges them at the right moment.
